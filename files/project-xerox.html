The Rise and Fall of Xerox

A case presentation tracing Xerox's full journey — from a market-dominating innovator that invented core technologies later commercialized by others, to a cautionary tale of market myopia and missed innovation opportunities.

Key Highlights

  • Evolution roadmap from Xerox's startup phase (1906–1959) to the present era
  • Analysis of the growth phase and innovation expansion (1960s–1980s)
  • Deep dive into the causes of the 1990–2000 decline
  • Examination of market myopia and missed innovation bets (e.g. PARC research)
  • Strategic lessons in adaptability for established market leaders
