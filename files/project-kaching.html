🚀 Marketing Leadership

Rebuilding KA-CHING — Marketing Portfolio

As Head of Marketing & Creative for KA-CHING, the Commerce Society of Ram Lal Anand College, I led a full brand and team turnaround — rebuilding the identity from scratch, running a 500+ registration flagship event, scaling to a 600+ participant annual fest, and building a content engine that grew the society's digital presence from near-zero to a real audience.

Key Highlights

  • Redesigned KA-CHING's logo and rebuilt the society's full visual identity
  • Built and led a 15+ member Marketing & Creative team for the 2024–25 tenure
  • K-Street: a 6-week themed campaign that drove 500+ registrations and 90+ positive reviews
  • Comverse: KA-CHING's annual fest, scaling the playbook across 4 sub-events to 600+ participants
  • Launched Ledger Folio, a dedicated WhatsApp channel for retention and updates
  • Built a recurring educational content engine (brand case studies, duopoly series, weekly news)
  • Grew Instagram from 600 → 1,300 followers and LinkedIn from 400 → 810 followers
  • Produced the KA-CHING annual magazine documenting the year's work
