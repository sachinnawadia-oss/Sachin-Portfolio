Key Highlights

  • Full STP (Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning) analysis

  • Audit of Dettol's existing ad campaigns and messaging
  • SWOT and brand gap analysis
  • Original growth blueprint: "Mera Gaon, Swachh Gaon" rural hygiene campaign concept
