📊 Risk Analysis

Geldium Delinquency Risk Report

An end-to-end credit delinquency risk analysis project for Geldium Financial Services — combining exploratory data analysis, a logistic regression model, and a proactive collections strategy framework to help the business identify at-risk customers before they default.

Key Highlights

  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on 500 customer records across 19 features
  • Logistic Regression model to predict delinquency risk
  • Identified poor payment history as the strongest predictor of future delinquency
  • Found high credit utilisation (60–80%) linked to a 1.51× increase in delinquency rate
  • Flagged high debt-to-income ratio (40%+) as a key structural affordability signal
  • Delivered both a full written report and an executive presentation deck
  • Included a Responsible AI framework for fair, transparent risk scoring
