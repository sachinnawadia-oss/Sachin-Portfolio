🧠 Marketing Leadership

Rebuilding Quru — Marketing Portfolio

As Head of Marketing & Creative for Quru, the Quiz Society of Ram Lal Anand College, I revived a dormant society with zero digital presence — validating demand with a low-cost pilot event, rebuilding the team from 10 to 40 members, growing Instagram from 550 to 1,500 followers, and scaling to Qurukshetra, a flagship event with 800+ registrations.

Key Highlights

  • Revived a society that had run no events for two years and had ~5% campus awareness
  • Validated demand with a low-cost pilot quiz before committing to a full recruitment drive
  • Scaled the core team from 10 to 40 members across Marketing, Events, and Content departments
  • Built a recurring quiz calendar (Indian Pop Trivia, FLAMES Quiz, India Quiz, Chandrayaan Quiz)
  • Introduced theme-matched visual identity and interactive Instagram Story trivia
  • Grew Instagram from 550 → 1,500 followers, crossing 100K+ views and 60K+ interactions
  • Led Qurukshetra, Quru's flagship event — 800+ registrations and 90+ positive reviews
  • Built cross-college networks with SRCC, Hindu College, Jamia Millia Islamia, and IIT
